Brainstorming


I started with listing down the five important categories given for the idea for proposals. Then I listed down all the possible ideas I could think of on each of the category. After that, I break down everything and grouped the ideas which best fit on the categories. I tried to eliminate some ideas that seems to duplicate and tried to make it shorter but straight to the point.
