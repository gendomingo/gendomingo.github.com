Game of Colors

How To Play

This game is set for two players. The first player will need to press the button then two random colors will appear. The goal is to display unmatching colors as possible to get score points unless black color pops out on either box. If the current player gets a matching colors, this player's turn is over, and it is the other playerâ€™s turn. After each press of button, the current player can either press the button again, or if the current player feels that luck is running thin, they can pass to the other player. And wait, there's a twist, if you get black out (two black colors appear), your current score gets zeroed out. The first player to get 30 points or higher wins.
