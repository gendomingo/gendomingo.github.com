    • about the last leaf

    The Last Leaf is a project that promotes an awareness to one of the environmental issues our earth is facing today, the effect of deforestation. The Last Leaf is inspired by a short story with the same name by O. Henry published in 1907. The theme of the short story falls for commitment and hope, which makes up the concept for this project where we should hope for wellness and sustainability of our forests and stay committed in saving it from deforestation.

    what is deforestation?

    Deforestation is the loss of trees and habitat in the environment and may also contributues to global warming and greenhouse gas effect. It may be caused intentionally or vice versa. Wildfires and overgrazing are a combination of human and natural factors which are examples of not intentional causes of deforestation; however, agricultural plantation, wood extraction, mining and urbanization are the common sources of forest depletion. Deforestation may result to global warming and climate change because it trapped the carbon dioxide gases in the atmosphere and no trees will help it absorbed. Forests are irreplaceable ecosystem, what do you think will happen if we ignore this issue and let this loss of trees happen??
